# AI Journey — First-time investor funnel audit (2026-06-10)

**Persona:** skeptical first-time Australian investor, $20k to deploy.
**Funnel walked:** landing → /compare → /advisors → /invest → sector /listings → listing detail → /go/ handoff — on desktop (1366×900) **and** mobile (390×844), plus a country-mode pass (`iv_intent_country=sg`) over /compare, /advisors, /invest and a no-cookie AU control.
**Method:** 3 goal-directed crawls (`bots/journey/ai-journey.cjs`, 38 pages), a deterministic funnel walk (19 captures, above-fold + full-page screenshots), targeted DOM probes for every candidate finding, curl retry-verification (4–5 tries) for every status-code claim. Side-effect firewall on throughout — affiliate `/go/`, leads and writes mocked; 0 real postbacks.

## The /go/ handoff (desktop + mobile) — healthy

`app/go/[slug]/route.ts`: 302 with click logging, per-IP rate-limit, `X-Robots-Tag: noindex`, `Referrer-Policy: no-referrer`, and a `/broker/<slug>` fallback when no affiliate URL exists. CTAs carry `rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow sponsored"` and `target="_blank"` (original tab keeps the comparison — good pattern). Firewall-intercepted clicks confirmed the wiring on both viewports. No interstitial; given /how-we-earn + per-row "Commercial" labels, that's defensible.

## Country mode — healthy apart from #6
