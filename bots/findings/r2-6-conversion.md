## Findings

| # | Severity | file:line | Issue | Recommendation |
|---|----------|-----------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | High | `app/get-matched/_components/TopMatchCarousel.tsx:68–74` | TopMatchCarousel CTA links (`hero.cta_href`) go through plain `<Link>` with no `trackClick` call. Affiliate clicks from the Get Matched result screen are **not tracked** — revenue attribution for this high-intent surface is blind. | Add `onClick={() => trackClick(hero.slug, hero.name, 'get-matched-top-match', ...)}` before navigation, matching the pattern in `QuizTopMatch.tsx:4`. |
| 2 | High | `app/get-matched/GetMatchedClient.tsx:276–296` | The `goBack()` function calls `/api/get-matched/start` again with truncated answers and **creates a new `plan_id`** on each back-navigation. The old plan row is orphaned. If the user goes back multiple times they accumulate orphaned plan rows, and the eventual `resolve` call uses the latest `plan_id` — dropping any resume/share token for the original session. | Pass the existing `planId` in the rewind payload; add a `/api/get-matched/rewind` endpoint that accepts a `plan_id` + truncated answers and returns the next question without creating a new row. |
| 3 | High | `app/get-matched/GetMatchedClient.tsx:332–338` | During the initial load the UI shows `"Building your plan…"` with no timeout or error path. If `/api/get-matched/start` fails silently (network error not caught before `setLoading(false)`) the spinner can hang indefinitely because the `catch` branch in `start()` (line 187) sets `error` but `loading` only clears in `finally`. **On mobile with a lossy connection**, users see a hung spinner and bounce. | Add a `setTimeout(→ setError({message:'Timed out'})`, 10000)` in `start()` as a safety net, and ensure `setLoading(false)` fires even in the early-return paths inside `start()`. |
| 4 | High | `app/get-matched/GetMatchedClient.tsx` | The Get Matched result screen (`ActionPlanScreen`) shows broker/advisor CTAs with **zero affiliate or commercial disclosure**. The `TopMatchCarousel` and the primary CTA button link to provider pages without any "Ad" label, `AFFILIATE_AD_TOOLTIP`, or `ADVERTISER_DISCLOSURE_SHORT` visible to the user. Quiz results carry the disclosure (collapsed `<details>`) — Get Matched results do not. | Add a one-line `ADVERTISER_DISCLOSURE_SHORT` near the CTA section, consistent with `app/quiz/_components/QuizResultsScreen.tsx:214`. |
| 5 | Medium | `app/advisor/[slug]/AdvisorProfileClient.tsx:1183–1209` | The contact form success state says _"Email confirmation sent to you"_ as one of the next steps (line 1193 array), but the enquiry API response (`/api/advisor-enquiry`) is not inspected for whether an email was actually dispatched. If email delivery fails silently the user is misinformed about having received a confirmation — eroding trust on a high-value conversion. | Either remove the "Email confirmation" step from the UI copy until delivery is confirmed, or have the API return a `{email_sent: bool}` flag and conditionally show it. |
| 6 | Medium | `components/BookingWidget.tsx:218–231` | After a booking is confirmed, the widget shows `"Check your email for a confirmation"` but there is no calendar attachment or `.ics` file offered. For a time-sensitive appointment the user has no structured reminder and may forget the slot. | Add an "Add to Calendar" link (Google Calendar URL or `data:text/calendar` download) in the confirmation state. |
| 7 | Medium | `app/quiz/_components/QuizQuestionScreen.tsx:208–213` | The Back button on mobile renders at `min-h-11` (44 px) which meets the 44 px touch target guideline, but the progress dots on mobile are **hidden entirely** (`hidden md:flex`). Mobile users see only a text progress label (`"Question 2 of 5 · 40%"`) — no visual indicator of how far they've come. Loss of spatial anchoring on multi-step forms is a documented drop-off driver. | Add a compact horizontal progress bar (already present in the desktop sidebar of `GetMatchedClient`) below the question counter on mobile, or make the dots visible at a reduced size. |
| 8 | Medium | `app/get-matched/GetMatchedClient.tsx:486–511` | The desktop right-rail "Your plan so far" summary panel is `hidden lg:block`. On tablet (768–1024 px) the plan summary is invisible and there is no mobile equivalent. Users on iPad or small laptops lose the anchor that shows what they've answered, increasing uncertainty and back-button use. | Render a compact collapsed accordion version of the answer summary below the question card on screens below `lg`, similar to the `<div aria-live="polite">` pattern already in place. |
| 9 | Medium | `app/briefs/new/BriefForm.tsx:150–215` | When `plan_id` is present, the form prefills and jumps directly to the `"contact"` step, skipping template / details / preference. The user sees no indication that 3 of 4 steps were auto-filled from their action plan. On error, they may not know what template was chosen or what description was pre-written. | Show a brief "Pre-filled from your action plan" summary card at the top of the contact step when `planPrefilled` is true, with a link to review/edit the template and description. |
| 10 | Medium | `app/business-finance/BusinessFinanceEnquiryForm.tsx:252–264` | The business finance form success state says only `"A specialist will be in touch shortly"` with no timeline, no confirmation email copy, and no link to browse related content. Post-conversion dead-end — users have no next step and may doubt the form submitted. | Add a response-time expectation (e.g. "within 1 business day"), an email confirmation note, and 2–3 related content links (e.g. "Compare business lenders"). |
| 11 | Low | `components/leads/HubLeadForm.tsx:125–130` | The "Your name" field has no `required` attribute in HubLeadForm. Validation only fires on the email field (line 56). A blank name is silently submitted, and the downstream lead object has `user_name: undefined` (line 89). This can cause confusing advisor-side experiences ("Dear undefined"). | Add `required` attribute to the name field, or at minimum validate name on submit with a user-facing error. |
| 12 | Low | `app/compare/_components/CompareDesktopTable.tsx:107` | On desktop the "Sponsored" label is shown as a small pill for campaign-winner brokers, but the inline tooltip explaining what "Sponsored" means is absent. The separate `SponsorBadge` and `AFFILIATE_AD_TOOLTIP` exist in `BrokerCard.tsx` but are not wired here — users on the compare table have no hover explanation. | Add `title={AFFILIATE_AD_TOOLTIP}` to the Sponsored pill, matching the `BrokerCard.tsx:91` pattern. |
| 13 | Low | `components/BookingWidget.tsx:167–209` | When an advisor uses an external booking link (Calendly, Cal.com), the widget CTA says `"Choose a Time →"` with no mention of what happens after clicking (e.g. how long the call is, video vs phone, timezone handling). The only text is a tiny attribution footer. | Add a 1–2 sentence expectation-setter ("Book a 30-minute call, confirmed immediately by email. Works in your local timezone.") above the CTA. |
| 14 | Low | `app/auth/signup/SignupClient.tsx:46` | After signup the user is redirected to `/onboarding` by default. There is no context on the signup page about what they are signing up for, what `/onboarding` contains, or what benefit they unlock. The page is bare email + password — no value prop. Users who land here from organic search (e.g. "invest.com.au login") have no reason to convert. | Add 3 bullet-point benefits above the form ("Save your quiz results", "Get deal alerts", "Track your portfolio") matching what the rest of the product promises. |
| 15 | Low | `components/StickyCTABar.tsx:56` | The sticky CTA bar on broker review pages renders the full `ADVERTISER_DISCLOSURE_SHORT` text only on `sm+` screens (`hidden sm:inline`). On mobile (the highest-traffic form factor) the CTA appears with no disclosure at all — just the button. | Show at minimum a single-word "Ad" micro-label on mobile, consistent with the BrokerCard inline "Ad" pill. |

## Quick wins

1. **Add `trackClick` to TopMatchCarousel** — 5-line change, immediately plugs a blind spot in the highest-intent affiliate surface (`app/get-matched/_components/TopMatchCarousel.tsx:68`).
2. **Wire `AFFILIATE_AD_TOOLTIP` to the Compare desktop table Sponsored pill** (`app/compare/_components/CompareDesktopTable.tsx:107`) — 1-line attribute addition.
3. **Add mobile disclosure "Ad" to StickyCTABar** (`components/StickyCTABar.tsx:56`) — prevents an unannounced affiliate click on the highest-scroll-depth mobile surface.
4. **Add `required` to HubLeadForm name field** (`components/leads/HubLeadForm.tsx:128`) — trivial, prevents blank-name leads hitting the advisor pipeline.
5. **Add booking time expectation copy to BookingWidget external link** (`components/BookingWidget.tsx:183`) — single `<p>` addition that reduces uncertainty before the click.

## Notable

- **No pre-conversion gate on quiz results**: the email-gate removal (commented `"email-gate" phase removed`) is architecturally correct — results first, then soft capture — and the `QuizInlineEmailCapture` is dismissable and post-value. This is a good pattern maintained correctly.
- **Get Matched affiliate disclosure gap is the most consequential finding**: the quiz results screen collapses disclosures into a `<details>` block (acceptable); the Get Matched result screen has none at all, and the `TopMatchCarousel` fires affiliate CTAs without `trackClick`. These together represent a dual problem — compliance risk on the disclosure side and revenue leakage on the tracking side.
- **Back-button creates orphan DB rows**: the `goBack()` → `rewindFetch()` → `/start` pattern in `GetMatchedClient` (lines 261–296) creates a new `plan_id` on every back-navigation, not just on the initial start. This is invisible to the user but causes DB bloat and breaks resume/share semantics.
- **Booking widget confirmation email ambiguity**: the widget UI promises an email confirmation, but no server-side evidence (API response parsing) exists to verify delivery — the UX promise is unconditional, the engineering guarantee is not.
