# UX/UI Audit — Business, startup, providers & monetization

## Scope

Routes: `app/advertise`, `app/advertise/packages`, `app/advertise/featured-placement`, `app/for-providers`, `app/for-advisors`, `app/for-advisors/pricing`, `app/provider-apply`, `app/providers`, `app/how-we-earn`, `app/how-we-verify`, `app/deals`, `app/pricing`, `app/plans`, `app/business-finance`, `app/business-portal`, `app/startup-signup`, `app/startup-portal`, `app/startup/grants`, `app/sell-business`, `app/partner`.

Components: `components/AdSlot.tsx`, `components/ABTestCTA.tsx`, and all pricing/apply client components in the above routes.

## Healthy patterns

- **AdSlot disclosure**: `SPONSORED_DISCLOSURE_SHORT` from `lib/compliance.ts` is consistently rendered in every direct-sold ad variant — good compliance practice.
- **ABTestCTA tracking**: impression + click events fire server-side via `/api/ab-track` with `keepalive: true` on clicks, which is the correct pattern for beaconing on page unload.
- **ProviderApplyClient validation**: inline, per-field, on-blur validation with `aria-invalid` toggling and real-time error messages is a solid a11y and UX pattern.
- **Deals page heading**: `<h1 className="sr-only">` correctly provides a screen-reader heading without disrupting the visual layout driven by the hero banner.
- **Pricing fee tables** (`app/pricing/page.tsx`): `overflow-x-auto` wrapper at line 241 correctly handles mobile overflow on the advisor fee comparison tables.
- **ESIC self-attestation legal copy** (`app/startup-signup/page.tsx:354–357`): includes a clear disclaimer that the platform "facilitates introductions only — no securities are issued or traded here", which is appropriate for a REGULATORY-AVOID-LIST-adjacent feature.
- **How We Earn page**: comprehensive, clear disclosure covering three revenue streams with explicit "What We Don't Do" negations — good transparency model.
